Medical Supply

Ship Provision Ust-luga is proud to offer a comprehensive range of ship chandler services, including medical supply solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of the maritime industry. As a trusted provider, we understand the critical importance of having access to reliable medical supplies and equipment while at sea. In this blog, we will delve into the diverse range of medical products offered by Ship Provision Ust-luga, your go-to source for all your medical supply needs.

First Aid Kits and Supplies:

Ship Provision Ust-luga ensures that vessels are equipped with comprehensive first aid kits that comply with international maritime regulations. Our first aid kits contain a range of essential supplies, including bandages, dressings, antiseptics, wound care products, CPR masks, splints, and more. We prioritize the quality and integrity of these supplies to ensure the safety and well-being of your crew.

Medications and Pharmaceuticals:

Maintaining an inventory of essential medications and pharmaceuticals is crucial for addressing medical emergencies and routine health concerns onboard. Ship Provision Ust-luga offers a wide range of medications, including antibiotics, pain relievers, antihistamines, gastrointestinal remedies, and motion sickness medications. We work with trusted suppliers to ensure the authenticity and quality of the medications we provide.

Medical Equipment:

Ship Provision Ust-luga understands the importance of having reliable medical equipment onboard. We offer a variety of medical equipment, including diagnostic tools such as blood pressure monitors, stethoscopes, thermometers, and pulse oximeters. Additionally, we provide essential equipment for medical procedures, such as syringes, needles, IV sets, and sutures. Our goal is to ensure that your vessel is equipped to handle medical situations effectively.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE):

Promoting the safety and well-being of the crew is paramount, particularly in times of global health challenges. Ship Provision Ust-luga supplies a range of personal protective equipment, including gloves, masks, goggles, face shields, and protective suits. We prioritize the quality and compliance of these products to help mitigate health risks and ensure a safe working environment.

Medical Consumables and Disposables:

Maintaining proper hygiene and sanitation onboard is crucial for the health and well-being of the crew. Ship Provision Ust-luga provides a wide range of medical consumables and disposables, including sterile gloves, disposable gowns, masks, disposable bed sheets, and waste disposal bags. We ensure that these products meet international standards for safety and hygiene.
